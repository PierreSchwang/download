What is the Heightmap Interface?
The Heightmap Interface is an interactive heightmap viwer and selector for Fawe.

  • You can browse and preview heightmaps online
  • The interface allows you to copy the command into your clipboard
  • Easy to setup and maintain
  • Supports browsable folders
  • Fully customizable
  • And lots of more features! Check the source
